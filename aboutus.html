About Us

Sukoon is a digital mental health platform designed to help users explore therapists, find the right match, and access support through a calm, simple, and user-friendly experience.

Our Vision

Making mental health support feel more accessible, calm, and human.

Our vision is to create a platform where seeking support feels welcoming rather than overwhelming. Sukoon aims to simplify the journey of finding the right therapist through an experience that feels reassuring, modern, and easy to trust.

Our Mission

Helping users connect with therapists in a clear and supportive way.

Our mission is to guide users toward therapists who match their needs, preferences, and comfort level. We want every step, from exploration to booking, to feel simple, personalized, and supportive.

Our Purpose

Designing a platform built around comfort, simplicity, and trust.

Sukoon was created to help users explore therapist profiles, take a matching quiz, and book sessions with more confidence. The purpose of the platform is to make the process feel calmer, softer, and more approachable.

Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is an important part of overall well-being. It affects how people think, feel, manage stress, build relationships, and make decisions. Seeking support is a positive step toward better balance, emotional strength, and healthier daily life.

At Sukoon, we believe that learning about mental health and finding support should be easy, respectful, and stigma-free.

Support Starts With One Step

Small actions like exploring therapists, taking a quiz, or booking a session can be the beginning of meaningful change.
