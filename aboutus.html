About Sukoon

Sukoon is a student project designed to help users connect with therapists in a simple, comfortable, and supportive way. The platform allows users to explore therapist profiles, take a matching quiz, and book sessions more easily.

Who We Are

We are building a platform focused on making the search for mental health support feel easier, safer, and more personal. Sukoon was created to provide a calm and user-friendly experience for anyone looking for guidance and emotional support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help users connect with the right therapist based on their needs, preferences, and comfort. We want the process of finding help to feel clear and approachable.

Our Vision

Our vision is to make mental health support more accessible and less overwhelming for everyone by offering simple digital tools and therapist discovery features.

Why Mental Health Matters

Mental health is an important part of overall well-being. It affects how people think, feel, manage stress, build relationships, and make decisions. Seeking support is a positive step toward better balance, emotional strength, and healthier daily life.

At Sukoon, we believe that learning about mental health and finding support should be easy, respectful, and stigma-free.

Support Starts With One Step

Small actions like exploring therapists, taking a quiz, or booking a session can be the beginning of meaningful change.
