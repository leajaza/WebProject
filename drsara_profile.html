About the Therapist

Dr. Sarah Ahmed is a compassionate therapist who works with young adults and professionals facing anxiety, burnout, and emotional stress. Her approach focuses on creating a calm, supportive, and non-judgmental environment where clients feel heard and understood. She helps clients build healthy coping tools, improve emotional awareness, and take meaningful steps toward greater balance and well-being.
